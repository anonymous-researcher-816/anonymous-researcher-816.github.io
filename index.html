VENUSX Benchmark Leaderboard

The first comprehensive, multi-level benchmark for fine-grained protein understanding. VENUSX evaluates models on residue, fragment, and pairwise similarity tasks to assess true biological knowledge capture beyond global properties.

Benchmark Task Categories

1. Residue-level Binary Classification

Identifies functionally important residues (e.g., active sites, binding sites) within a protein.

2. Fragment-level Multi-class Classification

Assigns annotated regions like domains and motifs to their corresponding InterPro families.

3. Pairwise Functional Similarity Scoring

Evaluates how well unsupervised representations capture functional similarity between protein pairs.
